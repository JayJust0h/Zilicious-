About Us

Zilicious Catering is a premier culinary brand led by Chef Zilander, whose 20 years of experience span internationally acclaimed kitchens and luxury hospitality. Trained in world-class establishments—including the Four Seasons Hotel Dubai, Mövenpick Hotel Aqaba, Jordan, and Rite Bite Dubai—she brings refined technique and global inspiration to every dish, artfully blending Kenyan, American, and Indian cuisines.

Since its founding in 2015, Zilicious Catering has curated exceptional dining experiences for weddings, graduation celebrations, birthday parties, and exclusive events across the United States—from Dallas to Seattle—and is now proudly based in Southern California.
